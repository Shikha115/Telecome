What's an Interactive Voice Response (IVR) System?

An IVR system is responsible to route calls to your contact centre more efficiently. It's an automated, self- service menu that's presented to customers whenever they will call dedicated support system. As soon as they choose one of the options presented from the desired extensions, they're connected with a representative who all are responsible in solving their related query.
